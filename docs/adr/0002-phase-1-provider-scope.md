# Phase 1 Provider Scope

Phase 1 prioritizes a stable, diagnosable bundle pipeline with YouTube working end to end rather than full provider parity. Bilibili, Xiaohongshu, and local video may remain skeletons or narrow adapters until their real metadata, transcript, comments, danmaku, OCR, and local-media workflows are deliberately scoped, which prevents early crawler complexity from destabilizing the core bundle contract.
